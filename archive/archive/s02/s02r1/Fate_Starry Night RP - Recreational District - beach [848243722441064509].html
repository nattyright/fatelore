Recreational District / beach
Being close to the ports, the aristocracy took advantage of the sea breeze and sea adjacent location to terraform part of the district into a wide beach. This sort of attraction has been valued highly by the elite, since Olympia very much lacks any natural habitats due to its huge amount of factories. The beach allows for several activities, ranging from sand castle making, seashell collecting to even swimming and surfing.
