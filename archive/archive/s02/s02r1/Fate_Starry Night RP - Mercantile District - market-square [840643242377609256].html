Mercantile District / market-square
A busy marketplace that could rival even the largest shopping center in the CBD. Made up of alleys lined with stalls and traffic that spreads in all directions regardless of where one is coming from, it's incredibly easy, but not totally unpleasant, to get lost within the market square. Fresh produce is the market square's biggest draw. Not only does Delphi source directly from its own indoor farms, it also uses its favors to strike up deals with farming-oriented cities for a variety of imports. Small businesses also set up shop here due to the cheaper rent and sell all kinds of exotic, handmade goods. It's not a stretch to say that the market square almost resembles the market in the Old World.
