Downtown / places-of-worship
The Church funded the construction of temples and churches within Olympia, allowing for free worship of any religion still remembered in the world, regardless of its origin or culture. This allowed for the workers to form strong bonds and camaraderie, not only through their tough, demanding work hours, but through their similar beliefs and ideals as well. These places of worship are also considered safe havens, to the point that the small homeless and low class population of Olympia is allowed to take refuge in them if needed.
