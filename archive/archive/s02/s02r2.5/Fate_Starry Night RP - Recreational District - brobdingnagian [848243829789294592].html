Recreational District / brobdingnagian
This gigantic cargo ship has travelled to numerous cities for more than 30 years and is now on the verge of collapsing. As a result, the aristocrats have decided to turn it into a battleground for the upcoming Holy Grail War. Brobdingnagian is a giant ship which has numerous rooms, cabins and storerooms, meaning that it is an ideal location for a battle between Servants. The battle will also be conducted at open sea, far away from the city docks to minimize collateral damage.

Despite taking part miles away from the floating city, residents of Olympia were well aware of the duel happening upon the Brobdingnagian. If one were to stand upon the docks at the battle was taking place, they would experience it all. Distant explosions billowing up into the air, leaving a pillar of black smoke to contrast against the white mist. A sing-song voice echoing outward, accompanied by a ghostly plucking of strings and banging of drums. The acrid stench of gunpowder, offset by an oddly sweet taste that tickled the back of one's throat. It was only after the cargo ship returned to the docks that people became aware of who the battle's participants were. Representing Olympia, was Archer of Ming. A man who was equal parts scruffy and refined, rude and polite. Every move he made was done with a mechanical elegance, every step purposeful and confident. One who seemed to revel in the opportunity to test his wits, and who responded to the curious gazes of the citizens with a smug smile. Representing Delphi, was Dramatic Saber. A peppy woman, with a body as beautiful as their movements. They stepped and skipped and leapt and twirled, as though performing on a great stage rather than fighting in a true battle. Even after such a gruelling battle, she had a wide grin on her face, as though there were no hard feelings. Shortly after the competitors left, the result of the battle was announced far and wide. Archer of Ming had been victorious.
