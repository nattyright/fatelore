Factory District / abandoned-factory
A notably old factory that has stood the test of time for an entire decade. Unfortunately, all good things must end as the factory was decommissioned and repurposed as a battleground. There are rumors saying that the closure was due to faulty equipment, resulting in the factory breathing back to life during random parts of the day. Some spectators theorize that the factory might even spring back to life during a fight between Servants.
