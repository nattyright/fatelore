This miniature district is filled with bars, clubs, restaurants and hotels. While relatively docile in the day, this district completely changes at night, allowing aristocrats to indulge in their worldly desires: The bars reveal their gambling machines, the clubs reveal their hosts and hostesses and the hotels offer their ‘love hotel’ service. The entertainment street is the closest you could get to a red-light district in Olympia, allowing aristocrats to forget about their world and melt away into a realm of pleasure.
