Recreational District / one-after-another-bar
A classier bar named after the way notes proceed in a song and to encourage drinkers to keep going after the first round. One of the more wholesome places in the Entertainment district, for those wanting a reprieve from daily life with gentle music. A competitor above was bought out by the de Allegro family, repurposed into a modest home for Lark who owns the place below. Home away from home.
