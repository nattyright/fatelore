Laurent MacKenzie BOT 30-Jul-21 12:48 AM
A few hours earlier... Laurent sat in his personal office, face expressionless. His mood was terrible, owing to Olympia's failure to keep their weather dome operational. Laurent had offered to send a few of his personal mechanics to help assist with the problem. Olympia's administration had refused him, saying that they had it under control. It was, of course, a lie. They just didn't want to be in his debt. Laurent's reputation preceded him, and they knew he was shrewd. They had known ever since he had managed to skirt paying several months of taxes to the Olympian government when he had forcefully shipped out several tons of products early, unfinished, so that he didn't have to pay the tax for having his ships in their harbors. As soon as that tax had been paid, Laurent's ships had come right back in, having swapped at a two-pointed city. He had dodged substantial fees, and when they found out, the law was changed. However, Laurent's headache was further helped along by the video he was watching on his computer. 'Alright, here I go-back up a bit more, you guys!' He flapped his arms at the eager crowd. 'I don't actually want to see you getting hurt if I mange to summon a really powerful Spirit, okay?' Laurent sighed as he quietly rewatched the video for the third time in a row. He felt stupid. For all his informants, all his men located on every corner of Olympia—and those finally infesting major population centers in Delphi—, he had failed to order them to check online. It was a procedure abandoned back when Laurent's informants checked for enemy business and law gossip, but now? It was invaluable. Laurent's hand twitched, and he slammed his fist into the table. Once, then twice, then thrice. He felt humiliated for letting this slide under his nose. His ego, as large as it was, felt deflated by this little pinprick. The phone rang. The president picked it up. A Servant pair, they're on the move. "Where?" He said.
Towards that ship moored outside Olympia, the one vacated for the Grail War. The decommissioned one. "Which?" That pair that's blowing up all over the internet. The kid who did that summoning video. Laurent's hand twitched as he hung up the phone. He knew what the voice on the phone was talking about. He took a deep breath, grinned, and simply sent Gustave a text.
