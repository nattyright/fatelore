Downtown / buddhist-temple
A lone Buddhist temple set on the outskirts of Olympia. This structure sees only one visitor due to the long and winding path to travel on. Jampo makes it his mission to travel to this temple each day after his work to pray and cleanse his soul, and to bite back the rising tide of Ashura that the work day brings out. Due to the secluded nature, Jampo has decided that this might serve as the best possible candidate for a Master's Workshop, and has decided to remain here for the rest of the coming War between Delphi and Olympia.
