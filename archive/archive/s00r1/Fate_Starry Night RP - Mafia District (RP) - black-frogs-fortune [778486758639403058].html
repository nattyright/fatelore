He would succeed though, his honor, his loyalty to the Yakuza he called family demanded it. He had reached the elevator, or should he say, the doors were already opening as he stepped in and withdrew a key from his pocket, clicked it into place and turned, he hit the 'Basement Floor 3' button and withdrew the key, the doors slid shut and as the repetitive soft tune began to play again he fell into his own thoughts. Was this coming conflict, which they had amassed frames and mystic codes for... righteous? He had no love for the aristocracy that rule his home with contempt for those below them, but would a conflict so openly with them help the people he thought they were meant to protect? Already the violence had destroyed homes and businesses, taken lives of those that wanted no part in the fighting. It wasn't his place to question, it was only his place to follow orders, to be loyal, yet still his thoughts wandered to the treacherous. The doors opened again, and he stepped out into the halls of the basement, here the grandeur was gone, there was only reality. Men stood armed and at the ready, he was sure a few master's and
servants sat just out of sight among the many rooms. Others would be stocked with weapons, ammunition and medical supplies, other floors were for uses… more sinister and gruesome than storage. He moved onwards through the maze of hallways till he saw it, a final door, it looked no different than the others, though there was about double the guards one would expect for such an unassuming door, he passed them and the door was opened by the last for him and he stepped in. The room was dimly lit, but there it sat, the S.E.R.V.A.N.T. frame was there, the mystic code prepared, the runic circle upon the floor drawn, all prepared by those far more versed in the mystic arts than himself, all prepared for him. He drew in a deep breath raised his right hand and spoke. While his mouth spoke the words needed his mind wandered again. Was this righteous? Was this honorable? Was he worthy? Would his loyalty hold true through this conflict, and with that last thought echoing forth he finished the incantation and his world was changed in a single moment.
