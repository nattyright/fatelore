Water District / the-commodan
A gigantic recreation of a Roman Coliseum floating upon the water, designed to host tournament battles for the Grail War, to provide tourists a chance to spectate, and to ensure that it is separate from the rest of Alexandria in the event something goes wrong. Originally meant to house a variety of different events, the arena itself is highly customizable in nature, and these customizations can be controlled by an offshore facility designed to ensure the continued operation.
