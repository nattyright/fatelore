The place was decidedly messy, like the windows had been left open and the wind free to scatter everything across the entire space as if it were creating a piece of abstract expressionism. Blueprints of houses and gadgets meshed with newspaper clippings, spare magazines and the occasional photograph. Clothes of all kinds draped themselves over a dresser and a pair of steamer trunks while books were stacked in every which way possible on the shelves: alphabetical, forwards and backwards, grouped by topic or time period. There was simply no consensus to how the place was organized.
