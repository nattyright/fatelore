nirvana / tropic-general
A tropical jungle designed to host exotic animals, ranging from monkeys to tigers and poison frogs. Since it is one of the more dangerous reserves, a lot of protective Magecraft and surfaces have been applied to the area, stopping tourists from exploring off-limits areas while keeping the animals safe from harm. Unfortunately, it seems like the system hasn’t been maintained in a long while, leading to the barriers falling as animals started to prey on each other. It is recommended to approach this area with caution, lest an anaconda coil up to you and gobble you up.
