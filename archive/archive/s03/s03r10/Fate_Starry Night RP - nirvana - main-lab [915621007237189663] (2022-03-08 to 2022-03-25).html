Abandoned laboratories that occupy the frozen tundra that is the heart of Nirvana. While formerly one of the city’s most prestigious locations, it has fallen in a state of disrepair due to a lack of maintenance from its original crew. Any staff or researchers who worked at the labs seem to be long gone. Instead, the labs have been inhabited by other Nirvana islanders. Some wonder whether or not the city’s own citizens still care for their world famous laboratories…
