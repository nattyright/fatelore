The Canopus / canopus-boxing-gym
A secondary gym on the Canopus with equipment geared towards combat sports. Being a smaller gym on the ship, the area doesn't see frequent use. The lack of use also means lack of maintenance, the floors are rather dusty and the equipment gives off a musty odor that might deter the average person. It's equipped with small weights, a standard issue boxing ring, and punching bags lining the walls.
