nirvana / the-rattlin-bog
Deep within the Nirvana swamp, surrounded densely by trees and water, lies the remains of a plane wreck. Half of it hangs (mostly) intact in the shrubs of a broken tree, while the other half peppers the surroundings with fractured metal. While uneventful and dead for quite a long time, recently, it seems as if there's been new life breathed into this old bird's grave... Named fondly the "Rattlin' Bog" due to the mangled plane's shakey footing, the area won't seem like much from the outside in. But make no mistake, there's more than meets the eye in this secluded bog. In truth, the clever works of Ronda Parker are at play here, making it a splendid place for Ryn to take as his workshop.
