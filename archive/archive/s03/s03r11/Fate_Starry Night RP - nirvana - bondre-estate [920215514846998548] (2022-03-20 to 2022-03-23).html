Between 20-Mar-22 12:00 AM and 23-Mar-22 12:00 AM
Alex Bondre BOT 22-Mar-22 07:19 AM
It had been a long while since the Bondre heir was last home in their estate. The normally quiet estate had been even more silent the past few days as Alex dug into the affairs of their fellow Nirvanians. A lone ice golem barged into one of the many unused rooms of the manor and began to gather up papers strewn about. With a stack of papers held aloft in its tiny frigid arms, the golem jogged through the halls of the manor eventually coming to a study room. Sitting at a desk next to a very tall pile of papers was Alex, combing over the details of some research paper. Alex's arm was in a sling, having been throughly broken by Ruth's servant. The pain was easily ignored, lost beneath the myriad of other sensations Alex felt at any given time, the damage however was not. Alex had considered going to Koji to get proper medical attention, but the Doctor's alliance to Ruth was up in the air and Alex wasn't confident that the doctor wouldn't sell them out to Ruth at the earliest opportunity. "Just one more moment and I would've killed that noisy harlot. That rotten Servant ruined it all!" Alex slammed their good hand on the desk. Now that they had the full scope of Ruth's plan, taking her out was nearing the top of Alex's priority list. However, Ruth was untouchable by Alex as long as her Servant drew breath. "Maybe I can point the invaders at her if Ashley doesn't kill her. She too big of a threat to ignore." Alex mused before grabing a research note from the new pile of papers, the other subject that was consuming Alex's mind. "Come on Mother...Father...what were you worthless scum working on? What weren't you telling me after I was no longer fit to inherit the Bondre name?"**
07:20
Once Alex rediscovered the drilling project and their parents' involvement, they began to gather up the scattered notes they had once so callously ignored in the hopes of gleaming some information. Through the emails, data points, and correspondences Alex began to slowly connect the dots. They had always knew that their parents wanted to restore humanity. That was what the Old Guard stood for and strived for since Alex could remember. However, the plan to do so was never known to Alex. Looking over the notes it was an ambitious plan at that. A marvel of engineering and logistics, a plan to drill straight down past the sea and into the very roots of the submerged Earth itself! However, what caught Alex's eye was a small detail in the plans, almost unnoticeable. March 23th 2102 The date of the Drill Project's execution. Alex leaned back in their chair at the realization. It was the same day Nirvana began their isolationist policy. On that day Alex sensed that the feeling of isolation began to take hold of Nirvana, yet until now they had never realized it. The words of Ace echoed in Alex's mind. "...you know, I'm almost compelled to think there's something controlling you," "...It's hard to believe that everyone just naturally hates outsiders for no reason." The more they thought about the more Alex was unable to refute those ideas. The collective consciousness of Nirvana was gripped with these ideas. The various moods of its natives rose and fell like the tides of the ocean. This was indisputable to Alex. It constantly blared in their minds. Were any of the Nirvanian's feeling or desires their own? Were any of Alex's? These thoughts haunted Alex that night.
