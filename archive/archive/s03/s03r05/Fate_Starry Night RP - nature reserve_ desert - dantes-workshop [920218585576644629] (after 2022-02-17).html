The desks and tables that should have been the same make and material as other similar items in the hospital were similarly changed to fit the Roman aesthetic, being made of wood and materials that didn't exist anymore in this day and age. Another detail to surveying visitors to note is that, unlike the rest of the hospital, this was used recently. Detailing the defence of the Canopus against Ashley and Caster, the encounter with Koji and Rider and the subsequent fight against Vortigern within the morgue.
