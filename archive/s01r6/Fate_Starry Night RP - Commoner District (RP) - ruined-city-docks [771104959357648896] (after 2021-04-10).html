Commoner District (RP) / ruined-city-docks
The Persepolis docks. Once prosperous, they are now a shadow of their former self. Ships come and go, yet always return empty handed. There are simply not enough resources out there in the empty sea. Most small ships that leave here never come back. However, since the emergence of the strange Noble Phantasm unleashed prior, the docks have been further reduced into nothingness. Little of value remains here.
